Title

Usage

bias(boot, theta)

Arguments

boot

A vector of bootstrap estimates of theta
theta

the sample estimate of theta

Value

z0 the bias parameter for BCa CI

Examples

X<-data.frame(rnorm(1000))
theta<-mean(X)
boot<-NaiveBoot(X)
boot<-lapply(boot, mean)
boot<-do.call(rbind, boot)
bias(boot, theta)
